Our Story

Brewed with sunshine, served with care.

Sun Cafe started as a tiny window on a corner where the morning light hit just right. Today, we still chase that same feeling: a quiet pause, a warm cup, and a moment that's entirely yours.

We source beans from small farms, bake our pastries fresh each day, and keep the patio open from first light to golden hour.
